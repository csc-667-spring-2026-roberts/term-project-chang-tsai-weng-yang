How to Start:

If you don't want the faced-up card on the left, hit the Decline button.

Otherwise, click Draw Discard and discard a card from your pile.

Draw: click Draw Stock or Draw Discard.

Discard: click a card to select it, then press Discard.

Reorganize: click a card then click another — the first moves before the second.

Knock: when KNOCK? appears, press Knock.

Game ends when a player reaches 100 points.
